# Customer Success Playbook for AI Tools

**Purpose:** Provide a repeatable framework to drive adoption of AI tools with clients.

## Steps:
1. **Discovery:** Understand client objectives, workflows, and pain points.
2. **Onboarding:** Deliver structured sessions, demos, and resources.
3. **Enablement:** Provide ongoing consultative support to embed AI into daily processes.
4. **Adoption Monitoring:** Track engagement, usage patterns, and ROI metrics.
5. **Optimization:** Recommend workflow improvements and advanced use cases.
6. **Feedback Loop:** Capture client insights to inform product improvements.

## Recommended Practices:
- Tailor training to team roles and experience levels.
- Start small, demonstrate value, and scale gradually.
- Maintain documentation and guides for easy reference.
- Collaborate cross-functionally with product teams to resolve issues quickly.

**Tip:** Position yourself as a strategic partner, not just a trainer or support contact.
