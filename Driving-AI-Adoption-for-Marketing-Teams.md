# Driving AI Adoption for Marketing Teams

**Goal:** Maximise adoption and ROI from AI tools in marketing workflows.

## Approach:
- **Onboarding Plan:** Structured sessions covering tool basics, key workflows, and first use cases.
- **Check-Ins:** Weekly or bi-weekly meetings to address challenges and refine processes.
- **Workflow Optimization:** Review campaigns and provide recommendations to embed AI efficiently.
- **Metrics:** Track usage, time saved, and campaign improvements.

## Example Strategies:
- Creating AI-generated content drafts to accelerate campaign planning.
- Using AI analytics tools to guide data-driven decisions.
- Voice-over production with ElevenLabs for video campaigns.

**Outcome:** Improved team efficiency, engagement, and adoption of AI tools.
